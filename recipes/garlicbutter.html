Garlic Butter

Garlic butter Ingradients

  • ButterThis recipe starts with two sticks of plain, softened butter.
  • GarlicOf course, you'll need fresh minced garlic (a tablespoon, to be exact).
  • CheeseParmesan cheese takes this garlic butter recipe up a notch.
  • SpicesThe garlic butter is seasoned with garlic salt, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and paprika.

Ingradients

  1. 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  2. 1 tablespoon minced garlic, or more to taste
  3. 1 teaspoon garlic salt, or more to taste
  4. ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, or more to taste
  6. ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper, or more to taste
  7. ¼ teaspoon ground paprika, or more to taste

Enjoy
